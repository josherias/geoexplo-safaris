Visiting parks to explore animals
Description

Today there are three major countries where chances of viewing mountain Gorillas are guaranteed and these are Uganda, Democratic Republic of Congo and Rwanda. For you to take part in this activity in either country, you must have a gorilla permit, and this is valid for just one day. In Rwanda the cost of a permit is $1,500, while in the Democratic Republic of Congo its costs $450 and the in Uganda it is $600. So choose a destination whose permit is within your budget.

A number of tourists prefer to book their permits and lodges online and then get on a bus to the respective parks, however this has challenge. Another challenge in having trouble getting around is that you may actually miss your outbound flights. So for that reason we recommend that you book your budget Gorilla trek safari through a well recognized tour-operator who will handle on the ground reservation even on a limited budget.

Although each of these countries has a variety of accommodation facilities ranging from the luxury, mid-range and budget, well considering the fact that you are on a limited budget, we recommend that you mainly draw your attention to budget facilities such as guest houses, budget lodges or backpackers hotels.
