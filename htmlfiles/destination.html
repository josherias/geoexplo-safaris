Uganda

The Pearl Of Africa
Description

Uganda the Pearl of Africa local translation “Uganda Ekkula” is a beautiful small landlocked country found in East Africa. Uganda admired by most is an Emerald endowed with the most beautiful landscapes, culture, wildlife and welcoming people.

Uganda is bordered by Rwanda, Congo, Tanzania, Kenya and Sudan making it that delicious steak in a sandwich for those that like to combine destinations.

Cradle of primates, Uganda is home to 3 of the worlds most endangered primate species these including mountain Gorillas, Golden Monkeys and Chimpanzees. In the Kazinga channel that joins lakes George and Edward, Uganda has the highest concertation of Hippos in Africa. Uganda has 10 national parks and 6 wildlife reserves. These protected areas are home to Uganda’s diverse wildlife and other world class tourism attractions.

Water bodies cover almost a third of Uganda while forest and woodland cover is 24%. Uganda is also home to the largest fresh water body in Africa, Lake Victoria which it shares with neighbors Kenya and Tanzania. This water body is also the source of River Nile with its source in Jinja, Eastern Uganda.

Other highlights that you should not miss when you visit Uganda include whitewater rafting, hiking mt Rwenzori, hiking one of the 3 extinct Volcanoes in Mgahinga, visiting cultural sites, experiential and habituation engagements etc.
